About ITC

The Institute Technical Council serves as the backbone of innovation at IIT Bombay. We enable students to build skills through structured mentorship programs, competitions, hands-on workshops, and constant support for technical teams.

Mission

To promote a thriving technical ecosystem that empowers students to innovate.

Goals

  • Encourage projects and initiatives
  • Provide structured technical mentorship
  • Organize technical events
  • Support technical clubs and student teams
